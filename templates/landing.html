Survey.

A self-hosted platform for multilingual research surveys, built on the SurveyJS form engine. Design a questionnaire, share a link, and download clean, analysis-ready data. Every account keeps its own surveys and responses private.

◈
Your own workspace Register in seconds. Your surveys and responses are yours alone — never shared across accounts.
文
Multilingual EN / DE / FR / IT in a single survey, with automatic language detection from the respondent's browser.
⇄
Balanced randomization Independent assignment pools with live balancing counters — ready for experimental designs.
🔒
Two-factor secured Mandatory TOTP (Ente Auth, Google Authenticator, Aegis…) with one-time backup codes.
