A self-hosted platform for multilingual research surveys — powered by the SurveyJS form engine. Design a questionnaire, share a link, and download clean, analysis-ready data. Every account keeps its own surveys and responses private.

How it works

Three steps from an idea to a dataset.

01

Design

Build your questionnaire in the free SurveyJS visual editor, then paste or upload the JSON schema. Multiple languages (EN/DE/FR/IT) in a single survey.

02

Share

Each survey gets a clean public link (/s/your-slug). Respondents need no account; the language auto-detects from their browser.

03

Export

Download responses as flat CSV or structured JSON at any time — including per-page timing and randomization assignments.

What you get

Everything a small research team needs, nothing it doesn't.

  • Private workspace — your surveys and responses are yours alone.
  • Multilingual — EN / DE / FR / IT with automatic language selection.
  • Balanced randomization — multi-pool assignment with live balance counters.
  • Per-survey file uploads — images, PDFs and shared choice lists.
  • Response timing — per-page and total, captured automatically.
  • Two-factor security — mandatory TOTP (Ente Auth, Google Authenticator…).
